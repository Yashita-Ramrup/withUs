Delete my account

Permanently erase all your posts, screening results, and account record. This cannot be undone (GDPR Art. 17).

Storage: Data is held in an encrypted SQLite database on the WithUs server. In a production deployment this would use a hardened PostgreSQL instance with row-level security, TLS in transit, and AES-256 at rest.

Retention: Data is kept for a maximum of 12 months from last login, then automatically deleted. You can request earlier deletion at any time using the button above.
